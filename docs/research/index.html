    Research

    Research

    Reading time: 2 minutes and 25 seconds

    Active research areas

    • Online guaranteed reachability computation for systems experiencing impairment (diminishment of control authority, changed dynamics). In particular, theory that applies directly to complex nonlinear systems is the focus of this research area.

    • Safe control of distributed parameter systems (partial differential equations), with applications to autonomous electrosurgery.
